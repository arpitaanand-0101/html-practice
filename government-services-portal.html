Introduction

The National Citizen Services Portal is a unified digital platform designed to provide citizens with easy access to essential government services. This portal amins to simplify the application process, improve transparency, and ensure efficient delivery of public services.

Available Services

  • Issuance of Government Identity Cards
  • Public Welfare Scheme Registration
  • Pension and Social Security Applications
  • Grievance Redressal Services

Eligibility Criteria

  1. Applicant must be a citizen of the country
  2. Applicant must be 18 years of age or older.
  3. Applicant must possess valid identity proof.
  4. Applicant must provide accurate and verifiable information.

Required Documents

Aadhar Card
Used for identity verification.
Address Proof
Used to verify the residential address of the applicant.
Date of Birth Certificate
Used to confirm age eligibility
Passport Size Photograph
used for official records and identification.

Important instructions

Applicants are advised to carefully read all instructions before submitting the application form. Incorrect or incomplete information may lead to rejection or delay in processing.

Application status can be tracked online using the reference numbers generated after submission.
