The Smart Nation Digital Governance Portal is a unified platform designed to provide citizens with seamless access to essential government services. It aims to reduce paperwork, increase transparency, and ensure that public services are accessible to every citizen regardless of location. Through this portal, citizens can apply for services, track application status, stay informed about public policies, and actively participate in governance through feedback and grievance mechanisms.

Available Citizen Services

  • Digital Identity Services

Citizens can manage their digital identity, update demographic details and access government-issued digital certificates securely online.

  • Healthcare Services

Access public healthcare schemes, insurance benefits, hospital registrations, and digital health records through a centralized healthcare service system.

  • Tax filing & Financial Services

File income tax returns, view tax payment history, and access financial assistance programs provided by the government.

  • Education Services

Apply for scholarships, verify academic crudentials, and access national education initiatives through secure digital platforms.

  • Public Utilities

Manage electricity, water, and gas services, submit complaints, and track service requests digitally without visiting physical offices.
