Conference Overview

Global Tech Conference 2026 is an international technology summit that brings together developers, researchers, industry leaders, and students from around the world. The conference focuses on emergining technologies, real-world innovation, and future-ready digital solutions.

Over the course of three days, attendees will participate in keynote talks, technical sessions, workshops, and panel discussions covering artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, cloud computing, web development, data science, and ethical technology practices.

Dates: 15-17 August 2026

Venue:
International Convention Centre,
Tech Park Avenue,
Bengaluru, Karnataka, India
